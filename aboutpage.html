About

Khalisa Syahba

Hi, I'm Syahba, and I've got a real passion for exploring. I once pursued art in hope to share things I'm most proud of. Over the span of three years, I did two art exhibitions in Jakarta, hosted workshops to share my experiences, shared loads of digital illustrations on social media, and had a go at different digital art forms, such as 3D modeling, animations, and video editing.
Although I still dabble in digital art every now and then, I've recently shifted my focus towards the latest tech trends. I've been tinkering with tech since 2018, tackling things like data science, web development, app creation, and even some robotics. I even spent three years working as a behind-the-scenes developer. But now, I'm all in on delving deep into the world of AI, which is the hottest thing in tech right now.
